What is Moonland Network?

Moonland Network is a Minecraft server for the Minecraft Java Edition. We offer unique game modes, a friendly community, and dedicated staff to ensure a great player experience. Join us today to see what makes us unique!

User friendly

Our server is designed to be accessible and fun for both new and veteran Minecraft players.

Up to date

Our servers are up to date and very unique. Join now under play.moonland.fun

Fast support

Our dedicated staff team provides quick and helpful support to ensure you have a smooth experience.

How to join

Here you can see, how you can join the Moonland Network Minecraft server.

1

Add the server

Go to Multiplayer in Minecraft, then Add Server
2

Enter server adress

Java Edition: Enter play.moonland.fun in the server address field and click 'Done'. Bedrock Edition: Enter play.moonland.fun in the server address and use port 25565, you should then press the Save or Play button.
3

You're done!

Now you can play on play.moonland.fun with other players!
Story about Moonland Network

Moonland Network started as a small server in 2021 with barely 20 players actively playing. Over time, thanks to the community's support and our dedication to unique, fun events, we have grown one of the most exciting new networks. We strive to offer a lag-free experience and innovative content for all players!

FAQ

Question 1: Which Minecraft versions are supported?

Moonland Network supports connections from Minecraft versions 1.9 to 1.21.10 to ensure everyone can join and play!

Question 2: Where can I find the server rules?

You can find the official server rules on our dedicated Rules page, linked in the navigation bar.

Question 3: How do I join the Discord Server?

You can join our official Discord server by clicking the Discord Server buttons found on this page.

Question 4: Do you offer a premium rank or store?

No, we don't have an in-game store for cosmetic items and ranks but you can still support us by donating some money.

Question 5: Can I play on Moonland Network using cracked Minecraft?

Only genuine, licensed Minecraft Java and Bedrock Edition accounts can join and play on the Moonland Network.

Question 6: Can I promote or advertise my Minecraft/discord server here?

Nope, it's strictly prohibited. Moonland Network is a family friendly server and we wouldn't want people promoting servers that might not be SFW. We don't appreciate people who join the server just to advertise.

Question 7: Why am I only allowed to speak English in the public chat?

The reason is simple English is a language that is commonly spoken worldwide that most people understand. Since Moonland Network is now a public community, there will be all kinds of people joining the server. In order to keep it systematic and fair, we only allow English in public chat.

Question 8: Can I get a refund for my donations done on web store?

Once you donate to Moonland Network, please note that all contributions are final and non-refundable.Your support directly helps us fund future development, server improvements, and ongoing maintenance.

Question 9: Is non-vanilla clients allowed?

Not encouraged. If you have gotten false flags/punishments by the anti-cheat while using any non-vanilla clients, support will not be given. Use it at your own risk especially for PojavLauncher users because we have no intentions for our server to be played on mobile.

Question 10: Why does the server keep lagging?

Most of the time, high ping is caused by a bad internet connection or a long distance between you and the server host.

Question 11: Is using an auto-clicker allowed in the server?

Nope. Using an auto clicker will make farming items easier and gives you an unfair advantage over other players. As a reminder here, do not drag click or butterfly click as the anti-cheat will think that you are using an auto-clicker.

Question 12: Someone is asking me for personal info, should I give them?

The answer is quite obvious. You should not provide any personal info for other players for your own safety. Staffs from Moonland Network will not ask for your personal details, unless we are solving issues for you in tickets. We will never ever ask for your password.

Question 13: Am I allowed to share my account with someone else?

Yes, but not encouraged. You are allowed to share your account with another individual. However, please be aware that sharing your password with someone else is done at your own risk. It is important to note that if the person using your account violates any of the server rules, we cannot guarantee an unban as the account holder will be held responsible for any infractions committed.

Question 14: Why didn't I receive a reply/Why is the response to my ticket slow?

Our staff members are not robots and cannot stay online 24/7. Most of our staff have full-time jobs or are students, and they may not be active during midnight. Please check the ticket rules before opening a ticket for more information on our response times.
